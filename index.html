Questions

Good to know

Is Libbi free?
Yes. Recording the heartbeat, trimming, labeling, and the core sound library are free forever, with no time limit. Libbi Premium is an optional subscription that adds cloud backup, sharing, the mixer, and more.
What does Premium cost?
$1.99 per month or $20 per year (about $1.67/month), each with a 7-day free trial for first-time subscribers. Prices are in USD; other currencies are set by the App Store and may vary.
Where are my recordings stored?
By default, recordings stay on your device only. If you turn on cloud backup (a Premium feature), they're securely synced to your private account so you can restore them on a new phone.
Is my data private?
Yes. We don't use ad trackers or third-party analytics, and we never sell your data. Read the full Privacy Policy for the details.
Does it work without internet?
Completely. Recording and playback work fully offline. An internet connection is only needed for optional cloud backup and sharing.
When can I get it?
Libbi is coming soon to the App Store. Check back here for the download link — or email hello@libbibaby.com and we'll let you know when it's live.
